Our story

Why we're building BookCircle

The best book recommendations don't come from algorithms — they come from people you know and trust. A friend texting "you HAVE to read this" carries more weight than any bestseller list.

BookCircle was built to make that experience effortless. We wanted a place where you can see what your friends are reading right now, gather around a book together in Campfires, and discover readers who share your exact taste through the Matchmaker.

Reading has always been personal. We're just making it a little more connected.
